THE 9TH FILM FROM QUENTIN TARANTINO

ONCE UPON A TIME IN...
HOLLYWOOD

This movie is a fun and classic in a highly modern way. It's a slow burn, that erupts into fun violence. A flashy and energetic alternate history that alsway inspires me to make more movies.

Follow washed up actor Rick Dalton (Leonardo DiCaprio) and his stunt double best friend Cliff Booth (Brad Pitt) make their way through 1969 Los Angeles: a city and industry they hardly recognize anymore. All with the looming threat of Manson family broiling in the background.

An expertly directed film from the visionary Quentin Tarantino, see history come to life in a new way, and watch it break down into wild, self indulging fantasy. Nominated for 10 Academy Awards (including Best Picture, Best Director, and Best Screenplay).

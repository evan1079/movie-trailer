Once Upon a Time... in Hollywood

Description

This movie is a fun and classic in a highly modern way. It's a slow burn, that erupts into fun violence. A flashy and energetic alternate history that alsway inspires me to make more movies.

Starring

  • Leonardo DiCaprio
  • Brad Pitt
  • Margot Robbie
  • Al Pacino

Art Direction

  • The goal is to remind audience of the colorful era of flower children and the last days of golden hollywood.
  • Tone is fun, gritty, bright and a little idealistic.
  • Sometimes graphic, shcoking imagery. Mostly classic Hollywood images.
  • Type is blocky, a little art deco mixed with 1960s modern.
  • Colors are bright, pastel and saturated.
